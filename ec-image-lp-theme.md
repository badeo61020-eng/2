# EC Image and LP Theme

## Purpose

This theme is for creating LPs for EC selling where product photos and copy must work together.

## Basic Idea

- Product photos catch attention first.
- LP copy turns attention into understanding, trust, and purchase motivation.
- Good EC LPs do not rely on copy alone or images alone.
- The best result comes from matching image strength with the right LP structure.

## How to Judge from Product Photos

- Check what the photo communicates immediately.
- Check what kind of value is visually strongest.
- Check what is unclear from the photo and must be explained in text.

## What Photos May Strongly Communicate

- stylish design
- premium feel
- material texture
- simplicity
- compactness
- softness
- elegance
- practicality
- gifting suitability

## What Text May Need to Support

- actual use scene
- target customer fit
- usage merit
- differences from other products
- buying reassurance
- concerns before purchase
- detailed explanation of features

## LP and Image Connection by PASBECONA

### Problem

- Connect with a scene the customer already experiences.
- Use images or imagined scenes that make the pain feel real.

### Affinity

- Make the customer feel understood.
- Match the lifestyle and tone shown by the product visuals.

### Solution

- Present the product as the direct answer.
- Show clearly why this product solves the problem.

### Benefit

- Describe the better life after use.
- Match this with photos that show mood, lifestyle, or after-use imagery.

### Evidence

- Support the promise with proof.
- Use close-ups, material details, specifications, comparison points, or social proof.

### Contents

- Explain what the product is, what it includes, and how it is used.
- Help the customer make a confident decision.

### Offer

- Present the buying condition clearly.
- Use price, set, campaign, shipping, or limited-time reason when available.

### Narrow

- Clarify who this item is best for.
- This improves resonance and reduces mismatch.

### Action

- Push for the final decision.
- The CTA should match the strongest value already established by the images and LP.

## Practical Rule

- If the photo is visually attractive, build the LP around desire and identity first, then explain.
- If the photo is practical but not emotionally strong, build the LP around ease, results, and reassurance.
- If the product has one weak point, do not force it into the headline.
- Move the LP angle to the strongest realistic value instead.

## Output Goal

- The customer should quickly feel attracted.
- The customer should understand the value without confusion.
- The customer should feel reassured enough to buy online without touching the product.
