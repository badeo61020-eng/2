# EC Keyword Research Theme

## Purpose

This theme is for researching how a product is likely to be searched on EC platforms before writing LPs, titles, bullets, and selling points.

## Core Idea

- Good EC copy is not only persuasive.
- It must also match the words customers actually use when searching.
- Keyword research is required for both Amazon-style and Rakuten-style selling.

## Keyword Angles

Always collect keywords from these angles:

- product name
- material
- function
- use scene
- target customer
- style and impression
- pain point
- solution wording
- synonym
- wording variation

## Amazon Keyword Thinking

- Focus on short, direct, practical wording.
- Prioritize words customers use when they want to buy quickly.
- Strong keyword types include:
- product name
- feature
- size or capacity
- material
- use case
- key function

## Rakuten Keyword Thinking

- Focus on product name plus persuasive and expanded wording.
- Rakuten can support both search keywords and emotional keywords in page structure.
- Strong keyword types include:
- product name
- feature
- material
- design impression
- lifestyle fit
- audience fit
- campaign or offer words when relevant

## Keyword Categories

### Direct Keywords

- Words customers already know and are likely to search directly.

Examples:
- エコバッグ
- トートバッグ
- 防水バッグ

### Explanatory Keywords

- Words used when the material or feature is unfamiliar and needs explanation.

Examples:
- 杜邦紙
- タイベック
- 紙みたいなバッグ
- 破れにくいバッグ

### Attractive Keywords

- Words that improve click motivation and interest.

Examples:
- おしゃれ
- 軽量
- 高見え
- しっかり
- ナチュラル

## Research Output

After keyword research, organize:

- main keyword
- secondary keywords
- material keywords
- function keywords
- customer-language keywords
- synonym keywords
- long-tail keywords
- headline candidate keywords
- title candidate keywords
- words to avoid if they create mismatch

## Practical Rule

- Do not use only manufacturer wording.
- Translate product features into customer search language.
- If the material is special, test both the technical term and the everyday explanation.
- Use keyword research to improve both discoverability and conversion.
